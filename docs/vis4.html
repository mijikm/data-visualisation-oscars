Movies & the Academy Awards

Vis4

The violin plot with jitter displays IMDb score/runtime of movies classified by genres. It is arranged in a descending order
based on the median IMDb score/runtime. Movies classified as Adventure genre had the highest median IMDb score of 7.9,
while those classified as Western had the highest median runtime from 1930 to 2014.
