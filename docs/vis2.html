Movies & the Academy Awards

Vis2

The boxplot shows IMDb score of movies that won the Academy Awards from 1930 to 2014 across awards.
The IMDb score ranges between 5.6 to 9.2 across awards and the highest median IMDb score of 7.9 is observed in Best Director, while the lowest of 7.4 is observed in Best Supporting Actress.
