Movies & the Academy Awards

Vis3

The heatmap represents genres of the movies that won the Academy Awards from 1930 to 2014. Among 20 genres, Drama performed best at the Academy Awards, taking up around 60%,
while Horror performed least taking up around 0.08%. A movie may have more than one genre classification.
