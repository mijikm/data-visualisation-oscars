Movies & the Academy Awards

Vis1

The bar plot shows the number of awards received by country and the mean IMDb score of movies by producing country from 1930 to 2014. Here, awards are limited to Best Director, Best Actor,
Best Actress, Best Supporting Actor, and Best Supporting Actress. Among 24 countries, the United States of America (USA) won the most awards, taking up around 73%.
As per the mean IMDb score, Malta, Morocco, and Poland are the three countries of which movies have the highest mean IMDb score of 8.5, though they take up less than 1%.
